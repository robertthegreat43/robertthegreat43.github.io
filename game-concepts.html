Ultimate Fantasy — Concept RPG

Ultimate Fantasy is a concept RPG documented in Ultimate Fantasy.docx. It features a dynamic Monster Map system, evolving regions, and a party-based tactical combat loop.

Core Systems

  • Monster Map regions with scaling threats
  • Party roles: Tank, Striker, Support, Controller
  • Faction influence and world-state changes
  • Exploration → Scouting → Engagement → Recovery loop

Crystal Palace — Cooperative Narrative RPG

Crystal Palace follows a group of lifelong friends who adventure together through a magical realm. The story emphasizes cooperation, shared decision-making, and the emotional bonds between characters.

Core Systems

  • Cooperative story arcs and shared choices
  • Faction alignment based on group decisions
  • Relationship growth through shared trials
  • Multiple endings based on unity and trust

Monster Map — Worldbuilding System

The Monster Map is a worldbuilding tool that defines enemy ecosystems, migration patterns, faction territories, and difficulty scaling across regions.

Why It Matters

  • Creates a living, reactive world
  • Ties exploration and combat together
  • Supports narrative consequences and faction shifts

Key Ideas
